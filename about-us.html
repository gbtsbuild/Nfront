About Us

GBTS Builders Solution Ltd (founded in October, 2023) is a trusted supplier of high-quality building materials and hardware in Nigeria. Our mission is to make construction and home improvement easier, faster, and more affordable for everyone.

We offer a wide range of quality products including roofing materials, hand tools, locks & knobs, security systems, appliances, and much more — all sourced from reputable manufacturers to ensure durability and performance.

Whether you're a contractor, builder, or homeowner, our team is dedicated to supporting your projects with reliable products, competitive prices, and exceptional customer service.

Our vision is to build a brand that is known for honesty, transparency, timelines and integrity in the building materials supply chain industry both in Nigeria and beyond.

Thank you for choosing GBTS Builders Solution Ltd — your one-stop-store for building materials.
